Phaedra PR+

Phaedra PR was added to our breeding program in 2009. Her show debut with Royal Arabians was in Las Vegas at the World Cup where she won her class with amateur handler Mark Davis.

Phaedra PR will be bred this year for the first time by Royal Arabians, and we anxiously await her foal.
