About Us

Royal Arabians is a full service Arabian Horse facility located in Mesa, Arizona. It is our goal to breed the best attributes of the Arabian Horse by breeding some of the best examples of the breed available today.

We believe the Arabian Horses we breed should not only be beautiful, with a distinctive, refined head shape and alert expression, but should also demonstrate the athletic, speed, and endurance qualities of the breed that make it one of the most popular breeds in the world today.

With a long history (over 4500 years) of having close human relationships the high spirited Arabian horse should not only be beautiful, but should also be good natured, quick to learn, and willing to please.

At Royal Arabians, our performance program includes Halter, Western Pleasure, English and Hunter Jumper training. Lessons are available for all age groups and experience levels.

Conveniently located in Mesa, Arizona, Royal Arabians is a beautiful facility ready to help you achieve your equine goals. For Information on our facility or our breeding program contact us at amcgown85@yahoo.com.
